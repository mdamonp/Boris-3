Boris 3 Galil Info

Translator IO Assignments:

Digital In:
1 - Jog Up (toggle)
2 - Jog Down (toggle)
3 - Pendant Select
4 - Translator Ready
5 - Installed at Station
6 - Translator In Position
7 - Stop operation or reset error condition
8 - Manual Home

Digital Out:
1 - Pendant Selected
2 - Translator Ready
3 - Amplifier Enabled

Analog In:
1 - Load Cell (Gain - (1000.0/3.0190) pounds/volt)

Motor Information:

Encoder Resolution - 8192 counts/rev
Screw Gain - 0.1"/rev
Max. Speed - 75 ipm
Max. RPM - 750 rpm
